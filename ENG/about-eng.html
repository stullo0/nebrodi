Our company

Nebrodi Costruzioni Generali s.r.l. , was born in 2009 when we bought the business Trovato Silvestro's company. That year we bought expecially the business concerning public and private works.

Our company claim over 50 years of experience in construction industry and also in the restructuring one. Over time we gain also experience in strengthening work and in renovation, recovery and enhancement of the monumental, artistic, architectonic and archeological heritage.

OUR VALUES:

In our company has a staff made up by selected professionists, specialists and craftsmen who work with great professionalism and passion, always respecting the art-work and for the historical and cultural values.

What skills did we developed?

  • - restoration, consolidation and maintenance of real estate subjected to the protection, recovery and restoration of stone elements;
  • -consolidation of land and special underground works;
  • -construction and consolidation of new civil and industrial buildings;
  • - construction, maintenance and renovation of roads;
  • - construction, maintenance and renovation of aqueducts and sewers;
  • - river, defense and hydraulic works;
  • - public lighting systems;
  • - special structural works;
  • - greenery and street furniture.
